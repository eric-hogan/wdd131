What is Disc Golf?

Welcome to the world of disc golf! A sport that blends skill, strategy, and outdoor adventure, disc golf is a thrilling game played much like traditional golf—but with flying discs instead of clubs and balls. The goal? To complete a course by throwing a disc into a metal basket in as few throws as possible.

Disc golf has exploded in popularity in recent years, with thousands of courses worldwide and a passionate community of players. Whether you're a casual player looking for a fun afternoon outdoors or a competitive athlete honing your skills, disc golf offers something for everyone. With various disc types—drivers, midranges, and putters—players learn to shape shots, navigate obstacles, and fine-tune their precision.

Best of all, many courses are free to play, making disc golf one of the most accessible and affordable sports out there. So grab a disc, step up to the tee, and experience the joy of the flight
